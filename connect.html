Customer interview

To understand the experience and pain points of the users, I have conducted interviews with 10 different users around the world which include users from age 18-23, mainly students and a few full-time workers 6 females and 4 males.
In this interview, the questions were based on what are their pain points. their experience using similar apps? what do they like/hate about the particular app they use? and what can improve their experience.

Planning

User requiment

Since the pandemic started and not been able to meet people in real. People have started meeting online and have been facing some problems like fake profiles, scammers, subscriptions to match with someone or being judged by someone.

Challanges


• Fake profiles - To avoid fake profiles there should be a verification process where the user has to copy a pose shown while creating a new account.

• To be unknown - So that the user profile keeps hidden by another user there should be an anonymous mode where the user can toggle between and be unknown.

• Avoid spamming of friend requests, calls and pictures by strangers - To add a stranger there should be a chat history with them and a user can't spam calls or pictures until he/she is friends with the stranger.
